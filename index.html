Michiel's Personal Page

I'm a MSc Marketing Analytics & Data Science student. I hold a bachelor degree in Business Administration. Having this page is a way for me to share my knowledge, ideas, and skills with regard to data science and (marketing / business) methods.

I hold three cardinal principles when it comes to writing, be it code or literary: (1) It must be approachable; (2) it must be rigorous; (3) It must be consise. Although such values are not particularly 'revolutionary', adhering to all three of them in the context of Data Science can be quite the challenge.
