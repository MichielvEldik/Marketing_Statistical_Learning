ARMA

Autoregressive moving average processes with order(p, q) where p = AR, and q = MA. Its a model that is used to describe a Weakly Stationary Time Series, so that is a prerequisite for it to work. * p = AR polynomial * q = MA polynomial The formula looks as follows: \[\varphi(B)y_t = \mu + \theta_q(B) \varepsilon_t\] \[X_t = c + \varepsilon_t +\sum^{q}_{n=1} \varphi_i X_{t-i} +\sum^{q}_{n=1} \theta_i\varepsilon_{t-i}\]

How to recognize ARMA?

  • ACF decays
  • PACF decays too
